An Ode to Frank

Helen Asimina Tosteson

My style is called Frank. It is based on the designs of the iconic Lisa Frank. Lisa is a designer whose vibrant styles heavily influenced children's media and toys in the 1990's.

I drew inspiration from Lisa's wild, rainbow-filled designs. I especially love her psychadelic dolphins.

In the map, purple hearts represent cities, yellow hearts represent points of interest, and teal hearts represent highways. Rainbow stars indicate natural land marks, and the major roads are rainbows.

And of course, we can't forget the cheetah-print ocean.

The map becomes more chaotic and colorful as you zoom in.

Take a look at a few different geographies, starting with Madison, Wisconsin.
